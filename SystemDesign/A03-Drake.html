1 A conceptual model was defined by Jeff Johnson and Austin henderson but according to the book a more reasonable definition is a description outlining how people can use a product and the concepts people use to interact with it. It is a strategy or manual to the concepts of a tool or product. The characteristics of the best ones are metaphors to show people how to understand a product and how to use it, how they are exposed to the product and the relationships between concepts and products.
2 The Model of the Xerox star is based on the classic conceptual model for the "familiar knowledge of an office."
3 The five types of interactions are
  • instructing - user give instructions. Typing in commands, choosing options, speaking and pushing buttons and keys.
  • conversing - Users have a dialogue with the system. Speak via an interface or the system replies through text or speech.
  • manipulating - users interact with virtual or physical objects. They manipulate them in some way.
  • exploring - Users move through a physical or virtual environments space. 3d worlds or virtual reality.
  • responding - User responds to system initiated interaction.
4 Don Normans three elements of the relationships between users and models.
  • Designers model - the model the designer creates of how the system should work.
  • System image - how the system actually works compared to how it should work. Shown to the user through instructions and manual.
  • Users model - The users understanding of how the system works.
5 Paperback book vs Ebook
  • -an ebook is easier to obtain and use, and most of the time is cheaper. You can even get a computer to read it to you. The only draw back is you may not retain the material as much and it takes up space on your device.
  • - a paperback book most likely makes it easier to understand the material and take notes, but its more expensive, takes up more space and its more difficult to obtain than a digital version.
Paper map vs digital map
  • - a digital map is easier to use, read, it reads out the directions to you, it notifies you of closures and accidents, but it needs internet connection to use.
  • - a paper map is harder to read, not up to date, and hard to use by yourself.
